ABOUT

Located in the heart of Slovenian alps, Pr Brus Apartments offer a complete expiriece of relaxed rural life inside a mountain village Dolina. We are located on the riverside of Tržiška bistrica, under the glorious mountains of Košuta range.

The sound of flowing water, the view on Dovžan george and lack of crowds enable a peacefull reset and opens the doors to many mountain activities. Apart from countless options for hiking and biking, the area offers options for sport climbing, bouldering, swimming and alpinism.
